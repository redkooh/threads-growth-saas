What You'll Need

To connect your Threads account, just grab your login cookies from a browser. It takes 30 seconds.

1
Open Chrome or Edge on your computer
2
Go to threads.net and log in to your account
3
Open the Developer Tools (right-click anywhere → Inspect)
4
Go to the Console tab, paste the code below, and press Enter
📋 document.cookie.split('; ').map(c=>{let[a,b]=c.split('=');return{name:a,value:b}})

Click the code above to copy it, then paste in your browser Console
