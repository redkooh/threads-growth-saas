Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes — no lock-in contracts. Your account stays active until the end of your billing period.
What does "daily actions" mean? ▼
Each post, reply, like, follow, or repost counts as one action. Threads take 3 actions (each part of a thread counts separately).
Can I switch plans later? ▼
Absolutely — upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes apply immediately with prorated billing.
